about me

I finished my Ph.D. at Cornell University, co-advised by Prof. Emin Gün Sirer and Prof. Robbert van Renesse. I also worked closely with Dr. Dahlia Malkhi. I focused more on fundamental problems of fault-tolerance, consensus protocols and peer-to-peer systems. My taste of research is to build the systems which are provably correct and practically effective. Currently, I am tackling challenges in blockchains, from a system perspective. I am looking for research collaboration and a faculty position.
