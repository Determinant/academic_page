about me

I am a fourth year CS PhD student at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Emin Gün Sirer and Prof. Robbert van Renesse. I also work closely with Dr. Dahlia Malkhi. I am generally interested in designing and building practical distributed/software systems from scratch. Currently, I focus more on fundamental problems of fault-tolerance, consensus protocols and peer-to-peer systems. In general, my taste of research is fairly simple: to build the systems which are provably correct and practically effective.
