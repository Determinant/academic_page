About Me

Ted is a third year CS PhD student at Cornell University, advised by Prof. Emin Gün Sirer, and also works closely with Prof. Robbert van Renesse and Dr. Dahlia Malkhi. He is generally interested in designing and building practical distributed systems. Currently, he focuses more on fundamental problems of fault-tolerance, consensus protocols and peer-to-peer systems. In general, he keeps a simple but vital taste: to build the systems which are provably correct and practically effective.
